Open Loops is no longer a collection of circles.

The entire canvas is a calm body of water.

Thoughts are represented as disturbances in the surface.

Users should perceive the state of their mind through the movement of water rather than through discrete task objects.

The canvas should feel:

- contemplative
- atmospheric
- organic
- slightly mysterious

Never:

- dashboard-like
- geometric
- rigid
- mechanical

The water should remain mostly still.

Only unresolved intentions disturb the surface.

Planning should calm disturbances.

Completion should restore stillness.

The emotional goal is to make users feel the difference between:

- active mental occupation
- contained intention
- psychological release
